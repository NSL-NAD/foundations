# AI Resources and Next Steps

This lesson is your reference guide — a single place to find every tool, learning resource, and community mentioned throughout this module. Bookmark this page and come back to it whenever you are ready to explore a new tool or dive deeper into one you have already tried.

## Official Tool Links

### Beginner-Friendly

1. **[Rayon](https://www.rayon.design/)** — Free tier available. Browser-based floor plan tool with AI assistance. Best for space planning and furniture layouts.
2. **[Spacely AI](https://www.spacely.ai/)** — Limited free tier. Upload photos and get styled interior visualizations. Best for testing material and style choices.
3. **[Nano Banana Pro](https://aistudio.google.com/)** — Free. Google's image generation model via Google AI Studio. Best for concept images and sketch-to-render conversion.
4. **[RoomGPT](https://www.roomgpt.io/)** — Free tier available. Quick room transformations from photos.
5. **[Planner 5D](https://planner5d.com/)** — Free tier available. Drag-and-drop floor plan and 3D design tool.

### Intermediate and Advanced

1. **[D5 Render](https://www.d5render.com/)** — Free Community Edition. Real-time rendering engine for architecture. Best for final presentation renders and animations.
2. **[Hunyuan 3D](https://hy-3d.com/)** — Free and open-source. AI-generated 3D models from text or images.
3. **[Kling 2.6](https://klingai.com/)** — Limited free tier. AI video generation from still images and text.
4. **[Autodesk Forma](https://www.autodesk.com/products/forma/)** — Free trial. Professional-grade AI-powered site analysis and environmental studies.
