## Official Tool Links

### Beginner-Friendly

**Rayon** — rayon.design — Free tier available. Browser-based floor plan tool with AI assistance. Best for space planning and furniture layouts.

**Spacely AI** — spacely.ai — Limited free tier. Upload photos and get styled interior visualizations. Best for testing material and style choices.

**Nano Banana Pro** — aistudio.google.com — Free. Google's image generation model. Best for concept images and sketch-to-render conversion.

**RoomGPT** — roomgpt.io — Free tier available. Quick room transformations from photos.

**Planner 5D** — planner5d.com — Free tier available. Drag-and-drop floor plan and 3D design tool.

### Intermediate and Advanced

**D5 Render** — d5render.com — Free Community Edition. Real-time rendering engine for architecture. Best for final presentation renders and animations.

**Hunyuan 3D** — hy-3d.com — Free and open-source. AI-generated 3D models from text or images.

**Kling 2.6** — klingai.com — Limited free tier. AI video generation from still images and text.

**Autodesk Forma** — autodesk.com/forma — Free trial. Professional-grade AI-powered site analysis and environmental studies.
