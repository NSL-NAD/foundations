# Floor Plans: Rayon

## What It Is

Rayon is a browser-based CAD tool designed specifically for interior designers and architects. It combines traditional drafting with AI assistance, making it one of the most approachable tools for creating and refining floor plans.

## Why We Recommend It

- Beginner-friendly interface
- 4,000+ furniture and fixture blocks included
- AI can generate layouts from text descriptions
- Real-time collaboration (like Google Docs for floor plans)
- Imports PDFs and images of existing plans
- Free tier available

## Best For

Creating and refining floor plans, furniture layouts, and space planning. If you've been sketching floor plans by hand in this course, Rayon is a natural next step for translating those ideas into a digital format.

## Getting Started

1. Visit rayon.design
2. Create a free account
3. Start with a template or import your sketch
4. Explore the block library for furniture
5. Use the AI panel to ask questions or generate elements

## Tips for This Course

Try importing your Module 3 floor plan sketch as a background image, then trace over it in Rayon. This bridges the gap between your hand-drawn work and a polished digital layout.
