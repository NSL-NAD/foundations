# AI as Your Design Partner

The world of architectural visualization is changing rapidly. Tools that once required years of training and expensive software are now accessible to anyone with a computer and an internet connection.

## Important Framing

AI doesn't replace the architectural thinking you've learned in this course. It *amplifies* it. The principles of space, form, light, proportion, and materiality you've studied are what make AI outputs meaningful rather than random pretty pictures.

Think of AI as a highly capable assistant who can:

- Generate visual ideas faster than you can sketch them
- Help you see your concepts in photorealistic detail
- Create multiple variations to explore options
- Translate rough sketches into polished visualizations

But this assistant needs *you* to provide:

- Clear direction (your vision)
- Architectural judgment (does this actually work?)
- Design principles (is the space harmonious?)
- Practical constraints (will this actually function?)
