## Best For

Concept visualization, turning sketches into renders, and exploring design variations. This is the tool to reach for when you want to see what your dream home might actually look like.

## Key Prompting Tips for Architecture

- Be specific about camera angle (eye-level, bird's eye, etc.)
- Include lighting conditions (golden hour, overcast, etc.)
- Specify materials explicitly
- Describe the mood and atmosphere
- Include scale references (people, furniture)

### Example Prompt

> "Transform this floor plan into a photorealistic 3D visualization. Modern minimalist style, warm oak flooring, white walls, large windows with natural daylight streaming in. Eye-level perspective from living room looking toward kitchen. Include contemporary furniture and indoor plants for scale."

## Getting Started

1. Visit aistudio.google.com
2. Sign in with your Google account
3. Select the Nano Banana Pro model
4. Upload your sketch or describe your vision
5. Iterate with follow-up prompts
