# Interior Visualization: Spacely AI

## What It Is

Spacely AI is an AI-powered platform that transforms sketches, photos, or floor plans into photorealistic interior visualizations. Upload an image of a room, select a style, and see it transformed in seconds.

## Why We Recommend It

- Upload a photo of a room and get styled versions in seconds
- Works with empty rooms (virtual staging)
- Multiple style presets (Scandinavian, Modern, Traditional, and more)
- Can swap materials, colors, and furniture
- Integrates with Revit, SketchUp, and AutoCAD

## Best For

Exploring design styles, showing "before and after" transformations, and testing material choices before committing. It's especially useful for understanding how different styles feel in the same space.

## Getting Started

1. Visit spacely.ai
2. Upload a room photo or floor plan
3. Select a style preset
4. Generate and iterate
